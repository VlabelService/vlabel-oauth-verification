Service Description & Technical Scope

VLabel is a specialized web-based application that:

  • Automated Label Extraction: Searches Gmail for Vinted emails with PDF attachments
  • Secure Processing: Uses read-only Gmail access to extract shipping labels
  • Format Optimization: Crops, rotates, and optimizes labels for thermal printing
  • Multi-Carrier Support: Handles labels from DHL, InPost, Poste Italiane, and UPS
  • Bulk Operations: Processes multiple labels efficiently in single sessions
  • Privacy-First Design: Temporary processing only, no permanent data storage
Legitimate Business Purpose:

VLabel solves the time-intensive manual process that requires Vinted sellers to individually search Gmail, download PDF attachments, manually crop labels, and organize files - automating this workflow while maintaining complete email privacy and security.
