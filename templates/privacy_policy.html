Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2025

Overview

VLabel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Vinted shipping label processing service.

Information We Collect

Gmail Access

  • Email Content: We access your Gmail account solely to retrieve Vinted shipping labels from emails
  • PDF Attachments: We process only PDF attachments containing shipping labels
  • Email Metadata: Basic email information (sender, date, subject) to identify Vinted messages

Authentication Data

  • OAuth Tokens: Secure tokens to access your Gmail account (when using Google login)
  • App Passwords: Encrypted storage of Gmail app-specific passwords (when using manual login)
  • Session Data: Temporary session information for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

  • Label Processing: Extract and format shipping labels from your Vinted emails
  • Service Delivery: Generate downloadable ZIP files with processed labels
  • Authentication: Verify your identity and maintain secure access
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality

Data Security & Technical Guarantees

Security Implementation:
  • Read-Only Access: VLabel uses only 'gmail.readonly' scope - cannot modify, delete, or send emails
  • Filtered Access: Accesses only emails from official Vinted domains (vinted.com, vinted.it, etc.)
  • Temporary Processing: Email content processed in memory only, never stored permanently
  • PDF-Only Processing: Extracts only PDF attachments, ignores all email text content
  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmissions use secure HTTPS encryption
  • Automatic Cleanup: Temporary files deleted immediately after processing
What VLabel CANNOT Access:
  • Personal email content or text
  • Emails from non-Vinted senders
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Other Google services (Drive, Calendar, etc.)
  • Ability to send, modify, or delete emails

Data Retention

  • Email Content: Not stored permanently; processed in real-time only
  • Processed Labels: Temporary files deleted after download
  • Authentication Data: Stored securely for service functionality
  • Session Data: Automatically expires after 4 days

Third-Party Services

  • Google Gmail API: Used to access your Gmail account with your explicit consent
  • Google OAuth: Secure authentication service provided by Google
  • Replit Hosting: Cloud hosting platform for service delivery

Your Rights

  • Access Control: You can revoke Gmail access at any time
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your authentication data
  • Transparency: Full visibility into what data we access and process
  • Opt-Out: Discontinue service usage at any time

Compliance & Verification

VLabel fully complies with:

  • GDPR: European Union General Data Protection Regulation
  • Google API Services: Google's User Data Policy and security requirements
  • OAuth 2.0 Standards: Secure authentication protocols with PKCE protection
  • Data Minimization: Processes only data strictly necessary for label formatting
  • Industry Standards: Best practices for data security and privacy protection
Google Verification Status

VLabel is designed for Google OAuth verification with comprehensive security documentation, transparent data handling, and minimal access requirements.

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@vlabel.app
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours
  • Data Protection Officer: Available upon request

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date. Continued use of VLabel after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
